Flights

Delray is in between two airports -- Palm Beach International (PBI) (16 miles N) and Fort Lauderdale International (FLL) (35 miles S).

Transportation

We recommend family and friends coordinate arrival schedules to share the transportation cost!

If you are arriving on Thursday or Friday, coordinate with your relatives for similar arrival times. Frank will help arrange complimentary pickups.

If you are arriving on Saturday, the wedding party will not be able to pick you up due to the early wedding rehearsal.

Write us if you have any questions!

  • Taxi/Uber: ~$60 from PBI, ~$80 from FLL
    (UberXL group rates probably cheaper)
  • Group Shuttle (Go Airport Shuttle): ~$25pp
    (Private cars with a wedding group for same price & only 1 stop)
  • Train: The Tri-Rail ($2-5 pp) goes from either airport to the Delray Beach, then it's a $7-10 taxi cab or Uber ride to the hotel (4 mi E).
