What to See

  • Downtown Delray Take a walk on Atlantic! You're staying at one end. There are shops, art galleries, bars, and more, galore!
  • The Beach Don't forget your bathing suit and sunblock
  • Arts Garage A BYOB theater for live music and plays. Check the calendar.
  • Kevro's Art Bar Wacky place. On "Best of the Road" TV Travel Channel. Live music, art murals, late night.
  • The Sandbar / Boston's
  • Palm Beach Archery For about a $10 cab ride, you can get archery lessons at the largest range in SoFla
  • Putt'n Around 1 mile from the hotel, 36 holes of putt-putt golf

What to Eat/Drink

  • Dada Go for the cocktails and tuna tartare
  • Coffee District Killer coffee shop that also serves a great selection of rare craft beer
  • Sazio Surprisingly great pizza and salads at a great price
  • BurgerFi Farm-to-table burgers fast-food pick-up style. Delicious.
  • Caffe Luna Rosa Best for breakfast/brunch. Across from the Marriott. Ocean view.
  • The Blue Anchor Nearby authentic British pub - i.e., it was erected originally in London.
