Wedding Schedule

The ceremony, cocktail hour, and reception are all conveniently held at the Delray Marriott in unique spaces. No transportation will be necessary to join in the festivities!

For those not staying overnight, valet fees are discounted; let them know you're here for the wedding.

The Ceremony

5:00PM • The Atrium Courtyard

The Marriott has a charming, shaded open-air courtyard in the middle of the hotel where the couple will be married at 5:00pm on Sunday. Please arrive at around 4:30 so the ceremony can start one time.

The Cocktail Hour

5:30PM • Villa Pool South

This private outdoors deck is on the east side of the hotel, so it will be pleasant, shaded and perfect for hors d'oeuvres and cracking the first drinks in sunny South Florida weather.

(If the weather doesn't hold up its end of the bargain, we have a backup location inside the hotel that is separate from the reception area.)

The Reception

6:30PM • Coral Reef Ballroom

This is when the REAL celebration begins! Four more hours of food, drink, and dancing! So, get your dancing shoes on, or take them off -- we don't care, as long as you join in this unforgettable evening with us!
